PRODUCT SCOUT

A recommendation system for e-commerce businesses

ProductScout is a software designed to help small business owners and investors stay on top of the market and ahead of the curve. By gathering data from local websites, we are adding machine learning to figure out what’s trending and why. 
By incorporating google trend queries as well we can more accurately judge how poplar an item will be in the upcoming months and suggest investing into the product for our users. By providing accurate market analysis ahead of the curve we can enable our users to beat giants and carve out niches in the market ahead of time.
How it works is as follows:
Data is collected using various APIs, Scrappers and Crawlers
This data is cleaned and stored in a database
A proprietary formula is applied on this data to make it suitable for use in timeseries model
Data is fed into time series model
Output is a popularity that is shown in the form of a graph

Proposed further working:
This output will be used as inference and be fed into a website
The website will display different items according to regions and display its past popularity and estimated future popularity
A mobile app will help customers on the go keep track of items
Will use containerization and docker to help keep website building professional and easy to maintain and upgrade
